# Introduction

This is a transformative era. The window of opportunity is narrowing, and those who recognize it are acting with unprecedented urgency.

## The 36-Month Window

Why 36 months? Because that's roughly the timeframe where the convergence of AI, economic shifts, and societal changes creates a unique opportunity landscape.

- **AI is democratizing creation** - Tools that once required teams now need just one person
- **Attention is the new currency** - Building an audience has never been more valuable
- **The old path is crumbling** - Traditional career trajectories are being disrupted

## What This Means For You

The clock is ticking. But here's the good news: 36 months is plenty of time if you start now.

> The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now.

### Step 1: Choose Your Vehicle

Pick ONE thing. A newsletter, a YouTube channel, a SaaS product, a consulting practice. Just one.

### Step 2: Build In Public

Share your journey. People connect with authenticity, not perfection.

### Step 3: Iterate Relentlessly

Ship fast, learn faster. Every piece of feedback is a gift.

## The Reality Check

Most people won't act on this. They'll read it, nod, and go back to scrolling. But you're not most people. You're here because you want more.

The question isn't whether you have enough time. It's whether you'll use the time you have.
